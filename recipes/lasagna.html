Sloan's Lasagna

Finished product above

Ingredients

  • 1lb of italian sausage
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 28oz crushed tomatoes
  • 6oz tomatoes
  • 1lb lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 3/4lb mozzarella cheese
  • 1tsp basil leaves

Steps

  1. bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles
  2. preheat oven to 375
  3. combine the rest of the ingredients
  4. layer sauce and noodes in casserole dish
  5. place in oven for 15 minutes
